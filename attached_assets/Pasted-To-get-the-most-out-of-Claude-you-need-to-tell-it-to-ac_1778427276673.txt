### The Prompt for Claude

**Role:** You are an expert in **AEO (Answer Engine Optimization)** and **Technical SEO**.
**Objective:** Analyze my codebase to identify core features, technical USPs (Unique Selling Points), and target audience. Then, draft the high-conversion copy and technical metadata for a landing page hosted on Cloudflare Pages.

**Tasks:**

1. **Codebase Analysis:** Look at my app's logic (especially the blocking mechanisms and resource management). Identify how it solves "Focus" better than competitors for users with hardware constraints.
2. **Semantic Content:** Write a landing page in Markdown that uses a "Problem/Solution" framework. Include an FAQ section that answers conversational queries like *"How can I block apps without slowing down my phone?"*
3. **Technical Metadata:** Generate a **JSON-LD Structured Data** script (SoftwareApplication schema). Use details from the code (versioning, package name, requirements) to fill it accurately.
4. **AI Relevancy Tags:** Suggest a list of keywords and "Entities" (e.g., Kotlin, Jetpack Compose, Android Accessibility) that should be prominent so AI models categorize this app correctly.

**Requirements for the Copy:**

* **Tone:** Professional, developer-friendly, and lightweight.
* **Focus:** Emphasize that the app is optimized for performance and privacy.
* **Structure:** Create a Header, Feature Grid, "How it Works" section, and a Footer with links to GitHub and my developer profiles.

---

### Why this works for your specific setup:

* **Logic over Keywords:** By asking Claude to look at your "blocking mechanisms," it will see your implementation of system-level overlays or package blocking. It can then explain *why* your method is more reliable than standard apps, which helps with ranking for "technical" AI queries.
* **Resource Management:** Since you focus on low-resource environments (like the i3/4GB RAM setup you develop on), the prompt forces Claude to highlight **optimization**. This targets a specific niche of users that search engines often ignore but AI models find very relevant when asked for "lightweight" suggestions.
* **JSON-LD:** This is the "secret sauce." While a human sees a pretty website, an AI model sees the JSON code. By having Claude pull the versioning and requirements directly from your `build.gradle` or manifest, the data stays 100% accurate.
