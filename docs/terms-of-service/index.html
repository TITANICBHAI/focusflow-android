What FocusFlow does

FocusFlow provides scheduling, reminders, focus sessions, standalone app blocking, daily allowances, blocked-word enforcement, overlays, notifications, and optional aversion deterrents to help reduce distractions on Android devices.

Permissions and device settings

FocusFlow depends on Android permissions such as Accessibility Service, Usage Stats Access, Appear on Top, notifications, foreground service behavior, and related system capabilities. You are responsible for deciding whether to grant these permissions. Some features will not work if required permissions are disabled.

FocusFlow is provided as a productivity tool and does not guarantee uninterrupted or perfect blocking.
